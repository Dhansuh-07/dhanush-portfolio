My background is unusual in a useful way. I hold a First Class BEng in Computer Science and a Distinction MSc in Cyber Security Project Management — and I've worked both sides of the security conversation: writing the policies and risk assessments, and shipping the software those policies are meant to protect.

"Governance only matters if it survives contact with real systems."

Alongside the academic side, I've led operations under real pressure — running a family business through COVID and supervising a 20-person team in central London — and I now build and deploy a production web platform. I'm looking for an entry-level role in Governance, Risk & Compliance, AI governance, cyber risk or security analysis.
